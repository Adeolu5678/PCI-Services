# Master Design Document (MDD): PCI Services LLC
**Aesthetic:** Technical Precision
**Prompt for Stitch/Antigravity:**

> **Act as a Senior UI/UX Designer.** Create a clean, technical, and high-performance website for "PCI Services LLC".
>
> **Visual Direction:**
> *   **Style:** Modern Technical. Clean, data-driven, engineering-focused. Use subtle grid lines and thermal gradients (Blue to Orange).
> *   **Colors:** Background #F1F5F9 (Clean Slate), Primary #0284C7 (Cool Blue), Accent #EA580C (Heat Orange).
> *   **Typography:** Headings: `Rajdhani` (Semi-Bold, Technical). Body: `Inter`.
>
> **Structure:**
> 1.  **Hero:** "ENGINEERED FOR EFFICIENCY." Split screen: Left (Clean Text + "Request Audit" Button), Right (Stylized Thermal Image of a Building - Blue/Orange Gradient).
> 2.  **Solutions Grid:** Minimalist cards with technical icons (Thermometer, Sound Wave, Flame).
>     *   Card 1: "THERMAL CONTROL" (Blue accent).
>     *   Card 2: "ACOUSTIC BARRIER" (Grey accent).
>     *   Card 3: "FIRESTOPPING" (Red accent).
> 3.  **Data Section:** "20% Average Energy Savings" | "OSHA Compliant" | "Denver Metro."
> 4.  **Footer:** Simple, professional contact info.
>
> **Code Output:** React + Tailwind CSS. Fast, accessible, mobile-responsive.

---

## Component Specs

### 1. Hero Section
*   **Image:** A building outline with a thermal gradient overlay (Blue at bottom, Orange at top).
*   **Text:** `Rajdhani` font for headline. Clean, professional.
*   **Button:** Primary Blue (#0284C7) with a subtle hover lift.

### 2. Solutions Grid
*   **Cards:** White (#FFFFFF) with a very light gray border (#E2E8F0).
*   **Hover:** Card border changes to Primary Blue. Shadow increases.
*   **Icon:** Thin stroke icons (Lucide React).

### 3. Copywriting (The Voice)
*   **Headline:** "ENGINEERED FOR EFFICIENCY."
*   **Subhead:** "Commercial insulation and firestopping solutions for Denver's most demanding projects."
*   **CTA:** "Get an Energy Audit" (Primary), "Contact Us" (Secondary).
