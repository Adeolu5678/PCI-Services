# Brand Identity: PCI Services LLC
**Aesthetic Direction:** Technical Precision / High-Performance
**Tagline:** "Engineered for Efficiency."

## 1. Philosophy
PCI Services isn't just about insulation; it's about energy efficiency, thermal control, and precise installation. The brand should feel like a high-tech engineering firm, not just a labor company. Clean, sharp, and data-driven.

## 2. Voice & Tone
*   **Professional:** Crisp and articulate.
*   **Data-Focused:** "R-Value," "Thermal Resistance," "Efficiency."
*   **Reliable:** "On time. To spec. Every time."

## 3. Color Palette
*   **Primary (Thermal Blue):** `#0284C7` (Sky 600) - Represents cooling/control.
*   **Secondary (Heat Orange):** `#EA580C` (Orange 600) - Represents heat/energy.
*   **Surface (Clean Slate):** `#F1F5F9` (Slate 100) - Clean, clinical background.
*   **Text (Dark Matter):** `#0F172A` (Slate 900) - High contrast text.
*   **Accent (Safety Yellow):** `#FACC15` (Yellow 400) - Subtle safety highlights.

## 4. Typography
*   **Headings:** `Rajdhani` (Google Font) - squared, technical, slightly futuristic.
*   **Body:** `Inter` (Google Font) - standard, highly legible, professional.

## 5. Logo Concept
*   **Symbol:** A stylized shield or hexagon with a "thermal" gradient line (Blue to Orange) cutting through it.
*   **Wordmark:** PCI SERVICES in Rajdhani (Bold), with "SERVICES" in a lighter weight below.
