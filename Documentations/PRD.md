# Product Requirements Document (PRD): PCI Services LLC
**Target Audience:** Commercial Property Managers, Builders, & Industrial Facility Managers in Aurora/Denver.

## 1. Problem Statement
Commercial buildings lose thousands of dollars in energy costs due to poor insulation. Facility managers need a trusted partner who understands the *science* of insulation, not just the labor. Existing competitors look outdated and unprofessional.

## 2. User Personas
*   **The Facility Manager (Analytical):** Concerned about ROI and energy savings. Wants to see "Energy Efficiency Audits" and "Thermal Control."
*   **The Construction Lead (Project Driven):** Needs a sub-contractor who can hit a deadline and understands code compliance.

## 3. Functional Requirements
*   **Hero CTA:** "Get an Energy Audit" (Consultative) and "Request Quote" (Direct).
*   **Service Grid:** Technical breakdown: Commercial Insulation, Firestopping, Acoustic Control.
*   **Trust Signals:** "OSHA 30 Certified," "Energy Star Partner," "Denver Metro Since 20XX."
*   **Performance:** Fast load, clean mobile experience for site managers.

## 4. Success Metrics
*   **Lead Quality:** Inquiries from commercial properties > 5,000 sq ft.
*   **Audit Requests:** > 2 per week.
